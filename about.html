About

A different kind of platform.

Built around a simple conviction — that the people who care for fine homes deserve the same dignity as the homes themselves.

ManagedHome was founded for households that have outgrown agency rolodexes and word-of-mouth referrals. We pair the rigor of a placement firm with the immediacy of a directory — and we keep both sides of the relationship in mind from the first introduction onward.

Every professional on the platform has been interviewed personally, background-checked, and reference-verified. Every homeowner is reviewed before introductions are made. The quiet result is a small, trusted community — not a marketplace.

We do not advertise. We do not chase scale. We focus, instead, on lasting matches that work for the home and for the people who keep it.

Contact

Speak with us privately.

For inquiries about hiring, applying, or memberships, write to us at hello@managedhome.example. We respond within two business days.

Privacy

Discretion is foundational.

Profiles never appear publicly. Background checks, references, and home details are stored only as long as needed to support the placement. We do not sell or share data with third parties.

For a copy of your data or to request its removal, write to privacy@managedhome.example.
