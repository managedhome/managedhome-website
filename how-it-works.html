A Quiet Process

How ManagedHome works.

An overview of the experience for both sides of the relationship — from first inquiry to long-tenured service.

For Homeowners

From inquiry to introduction.

I

Create an account

Tell us about your household, residences, and the position you wish to fill.

II

Browse curated profiles

Each candidate has been interviewed, background-checked, and reference-verified.

III

Request introductions

Schedule meetings privately. We help coordinate trials and finalize terms.

For Professionals

From application to placement.

I

Apply

Submit a complete profile. We accept fewer than one in eight applicants.

II

Be vetted

Personal interview, references, and credential review — handled discreetly.

III

Be matched

Families reach out through your private dashboard. You are always in control.

Standards

What we expect.

Background Checks

Every professional undergoes a comprehensive background check, identity verification, and credential review.

Reference Verification

A minimum of two prior employers must speak personally to our placement team.

Discretion

Profiles never appear publicly. Inquiries are made through the platform, never published.

A Lasting Match

We focus on long-term placements. Our average tenure exceeds four years.
